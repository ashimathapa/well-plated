Well Plated

Burger

Ingredients Required: Cheese, tomato, ground beef, salt, black pepper,onion ,lettuce

Recipe:

  • Step 1: Mix 1 lb ground beef with 1 tsp salt and 1/2 tsp black pepper.
  • Step 2: Shape into patties and grill or pan-fry until cooked to your liking.
  • Step 3: Toast burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  • Step 4: Assemble the burgers: Place lettuce, tomato slices, and onion rings on the bottom bun.
  • Step 5: Add the cooked beef patty and your choice of cheese (optional).
  • Step 6: Top with condiments like ketchup, mustard, mayo, and pickles, then cover with the top bun. Enjoy!
