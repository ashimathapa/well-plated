Pulao

Ingredients Required: Rice, Ghee, Dry Fruits, vegetables, salt

Recipe:

  • Step 1: Rinse 1 cup of basmati rice and soak for 30 minutes, then drain.
  • Step 2: Heat 2-3 tablespoons of oil or ghee in a pot, add 1-2 teaspoons of whole spices and sauté.
  • Step 3: Add 1 large thinly sliced onion, cook until golden brown.
  • Step 4: Mix in 1 tablespoon of ginger-garlic paste and chopped vegetables, sauté for a few minutes.
  • Step 5: Add the soaked rice, 2 cups of water or broth, salt, and bring to a boil, then simmer with a lid on until rice is cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  • Step 6: Optionally, garnish with nuts and raisins. Serve hot and enjoy your pulao!
