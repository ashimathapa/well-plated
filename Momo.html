Well Plated

Momo

Ingredients Required: Flour, meat or vegetables,salt, water, onion,oil ginger, soy sauce

Recipe:

  • Step 1: Make dough by mixing 2 cups flour, water, and a pinch of salt. Rest for 30 minutes.
  • Step 2: Mix 1 cup chopped cabbage, 1/2 cup chopped onions, ginger, 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tbsp sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  • Step 3: Roll small dough portions into thin circles.
  • Step 4: Place veggie filling in the center, fold, and seal edges
  • Step 5: Steam momo for 10-12 mins until cooked.
  • Step 6: Serve hot with dipping sauce. Enjoy!
